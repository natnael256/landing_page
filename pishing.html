Phishing Awareness Training

You've been part of a simulated phishing attack.

Warning: Always be cautious of unexpected emails, especially those asking for personal information or login credentials.

Here are some tips to identify phishing emails:

  • Check the sender's email address for suspicious or misspelled domains.
  • Hover over links to verify they lead to legitimate websites.
  • Look for spelling or grammatical errors in the email.
  • Be wary of urgent or threatening language.

Remember, your vigilance is crucial in protecting our organization from cyber threats.
